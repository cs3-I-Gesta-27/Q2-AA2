PHILIPPINE SCIENCE HIGH SCHOOL - CARAGA REGION CAMPUS


FACILITIES

The Philippine Science High School - Caraga Region Campus is located at Brgy. Ampayon, Butuan City, Agusan Del Norte. The PSHS-CRC facilities provide a better educational experience by giving students their needs that help them achieve an excellent school and in life experience.

Gymnasium

The Gymnasium is used for PE classes. It is also where the student's major events are held.

Academic Bldg. 1


Academic Building 1 is where grade 7-10 students' classrooms are located. Clinic, Registrar, Guidance office can also be found in the first floor.

Academic Bldg. 4


Academic building 4 is where the grade 11 and 12 students' classrooms and faculty offices are located.

Library


The library is where students study and go whenever they need WiFi for their researches and school-related assessments. The library also provides a lot of books for students to read.

Fabrication Lab


The fabrication lab is a two-storey building with a terrace. Wherein the first floor is occupied as the fabrication lab where fabrication happen for engineering students, while the second floor is occupied as an ADTech room for grade 7 and 8 student.

Canteen and Students Activity Center


This three-storey building is where both the canteen and students activity center are located. The canteen occupies both fisrt and second floor, while SAC occupies third floor where music room and dance room are located

Admin Bldg.


The Admin Building is where the office of the Campus Director and staffs are located.
